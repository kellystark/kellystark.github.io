About

Kelly is a UX/UI Designer based in Omaha, NE. She combines her skills as an author, a digital artist, and a front-end developer to create great user experiences. She also uses her psychology degree she earned from Peru State to help her understand what drives users to certain behaviors.

Her goal is to further her knowledge and experience in UX/UI to make it an integral part of her design process.
